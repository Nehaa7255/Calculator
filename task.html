Indian Army

The renowned history of the Indian Army dates back more than ten thousand years. Ramayana and Mahabharata are the two grand epics that constitute the fundamental framework around which the edifice of the Indian Army is built.
It originated from the armies of the East India Company and in due course became the British Indian Army and the armies of the princely states. The armies of Bengal, Bombay, and Madras presidencies were merged to form a unified Indian Army in 1895.
